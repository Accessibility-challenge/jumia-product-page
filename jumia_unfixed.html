Product details

Samsung Galaxy Z Fold6 comes with a 120 Hz refresh rate 7.6-inch touchscreen primary display offering a resolution of 1856x2160 pixels at a pixel density of 374 pixels per inch (ppi). The display sports Gorilla Glass Victus 2 for protection. It also features a 6.3-inch touchscreen as its second display, with a resolution of 968x2376 pixels at a pixel density of 412 pixels per inch (ppi). Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 6 is powered by an octa-core Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 processor. It comes with 12GB of RAM. The Galaxy Z Fold 6 runs Android 14 and is powered by a 4400mAh battery. The Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 6 supports wireless charging, as well as proprietary fast charging.

As far as the cameras are concerned, the Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 6 on the rear packs a triple camera setup featuring a 50-megapixel (f/1.8) primary camera; a 12-megapixel (f/2.2, ultra-wide-angle) camera, and a 10-megapixel (f/2.4, telephoto) camera. The rear camera setup has autofocus. On the front, the Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 6 packs a dual camera setup for selfies, featuring a 10-megapixel primary camera with an f/2.2 aperture and a second 4-megapixel camera with an f/1.8 aperture and a pixel size of 2.0-micron.

The Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 6 runs One UI 6.1.1 is based on Android 14 and packs 256GB, 512GB, 1TB of inbuilt storage. 

 

Connectivity options on the Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 6 include Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac/ax, GPS, Bluetooth v5.3, NFC, USB Type-C. Sensors on the phone include accelerometer, ambient light sensor, barometer, compass/ magnetometer, gyroscope, proximity sensor, and fingerprint sensor.
